📄 Save as PDF on iPhone

    

      
Safari on iPhone doesn't support direct printing. Follow these steps:

      
1
Tap the Share button □↑
Bottom of Safari screen (top-right on iPad).

      
2
Scroll down and tap Print

      
3
Pinch outward on the page preview
Zoom gesture converts it to a PDF.

      
4
Tap Share □↑ → Save to Files

      

    

  







  

    
📄 Save as PDF on Android

    

      
Chrome on Android has a built-in Save to PDF feature:

      
1
Tap ⋮ menu (top right of Chrome)

      
2
Tap Share → Print

      
3
Select Save as PDF as the printer

      
4
Tap Download / Save
