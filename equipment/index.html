📄 Save as PDF on iPhone

    

      
Safari on iPhone doesn't print directly. Save a PDF in four taps:

      

        
1

        
Tap the Share button
The icon at the bottom of Safari (or top-right on iPad).

      

      

        
2

        
Scroll down and tap "Print"
In the share sheet, scroll until you see Print.

      

      

        
3

        
Pinch open the page preview
Pinch outward on the thumbnail — this converts it to PDF.

      

      

        
4

        
Share → Save to Files
A new share sheet appears. Save to Files or send via email / WhatsApp.

      

      

        
        
      

    

  







  

    
📄 Save as PDF on Android

    

      
Chrome on Android has a built-in Save to PDF feature:

      

        
1

        
Tap the ⋮ menu (top right)
Three-dot menu in Chrome.

      

      

        
2

        
Tap "Share" → "Print"

      

      

        
3

        
Select "Save as PDF" as the printer

      

      

        
4

        
Tap Download / Save
Saves to Downloads.
